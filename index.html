What is Web Development?

Web development is the process of creating websites and applications for the internet. It encompasses a variety of tasks and disciplines, including web design, web publishing, web programming, and database management. Whether you're building a simple static page or a complex web application, understanding the fundamentals of web development is essential.

Why Code?

Coding empowers you to bring your ideas to life on the web. Here are some reasons why learning to code is beneficial:

  • Creativity: Design and develop unique websites and applications.
  • Problem-Solving: Enhance your analytical and logical thinking skills.
  • Career Opportunities: Open doors to a wide range of job prospects in technology.
  • Autonomy: Build and maintain your own projects without relying on others.
  • Continuous Learning: Stay updated with evolving technologies and frameworks.

Where Do I Start?

Embarking on your web development journey can be exciting and overwhelming. Here's a roadmap to get you started:

  1. Learn HTML: Understand the structure of web pages.
  2. Learn CSS: Master styling and layout techniques.
  3. Learn JavaScript: Add interactivity and dynamic behavior.
  4. Explore Frameworks: Get familiar with libraries and frameworks like Bootstrap.
  5. Build Projects: Apply your knowledge by creating real-world projects.
  6. Version Control: Learn Git and GitHub for tracking changes and collaboration.
  7. Continuous Learning: Stay updated with the latest trends and technologies.
